About Us

Independent advisory for responsible deployment of capital in Syria.

Who We Are

We enable responsible capital deployment into Syria’s private sector with discipline, transparency, and local partnership.

Syrian Consultancy Group is an independent advisory and investment facilitation firm focused on connecting global investors with vetted Syrian businesses. We combine frontier-market expertise with international best practices to structure partnerships that are both compliant and catalytic.

Our Principles

  1. Compliance First: KYC/AML, sanctions screening, and legal clarity.
  2. Transparency: Clear terms, clear risks, clear reporting.
  3. Impact: Measurable outcomes for jobs and livelihoods.
  4. Local Partnership: Centering Syrian talent and context.
